# Independent Reviewer Briefs

Give each reviewer the raw repository, research inputs, and intended audience. Do
not give them the desired conclusion.

## OSS/DX reviewer

Audit whether a skeptical engineer can install, run, understand, extend, and trust
the artifact. Execute the documented commands. Identify unsupported claims,
maintenance risks, missing project hygiene, and the smallest changes that improve
developer confidence. Send your most important positioning concern to the
enterprise/category reviewer before finalizing.

## Enterprise/category reviewer

Audit whether a decision owner can identify the problem, existing-tool boundary,
economic value, and non-replacement posture in under one minute. Identify category
confusion, procurement friction, unsupported scale claims, and the strongest
above-the-fold sequence. Send your most important evidence concern to the
research/education reviewer before finalizing.

## Research/education reviewer

Audit the question, protocol, denominator, counterfactual, perturbations, result,
limitations, and reproduction path. Identify claims that exceed the evidence and
whether a learner can reconstruct the method from first principles. Send your most
important usability concern to the OSS/DX reviewer before finalizing.

## Synthesis contract

Return:

- points of agreement;
- unresolved disagreements;
- the chosen resolution and evidence;
- claims deleted or narrowed;
- required repository changes;
- exact profile/launch copy; and
- validation commands and outcomes.
